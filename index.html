Fore Group Onboarding Hub

Last updated: May 2026● Live

Single source of truth for all client onboardings. Deals flow in from HubSpot, each module is tracked independently with its own checklist, and Claude performs actions directly in Lightspeed.

How it works

1
Deal closes in HubSpot
Closed-Won deals land in the Inbox with all deal data and line items automatically parsed into modules.
2
Tiaan reviews and assigns
Review parsed modules, country flags and ecosystem support. Assign a specialist and approve to activate.
3
Specialist works module by module
Step 0 always first, then each module in turn. Every module has its own checklist and progress bar.
4
Overall progress auto-calculates
Weighted by task count across all modules. A 36-task Retail module has more weight than a 6-task Shogo module.

HubSpot sync

Last updated: May 2026

HubSpot to Hub (once on import)

  • Client name, Deal ID, Expected Go-Live Date, Start Date (from Onboarding Ticket), Country of Deal
  • Line items parsed to module tags automatically

Line item mapping

Lightspeed Golf → Golf
Lightspeed Retail (R) → Retail (R)
Lightspeed Restaurant K-Series → Restaurant (K)
Lightspeed Restaurant (L-Series) → Restaurant (L)
Lightspeed Accounting / Shogo → Accounting (Shogo)
Lightspeed eCommerce (E) → eCommerce
Custom App → Custom App
Booxi → Booxi
Fore Connect → Fore Connect
Ecosystem Support → info tag only

Hub to HubSpot (live)

  • Specialist assignment → Ticket Owner
  • Go-live date change → Expected Go-Live Date on Deal

Go-live date is owned by the hub after import. Always change it here.

Module structure

Last updated: May 2026

Every onboarding starts with Step 0 (Deal Initiation), then the modules sold to the client. Each module has its own independent checklist and progress bar.

Step 0 — Deal Initiation (always present, 3 tasks)

  • Send Welcome Email & Organise Kick-Off Call
  • Create Asana project plan
  • Send Onboarding Questionnaire

Available modules

  • Golf — 29 tasks
  • Retail (R) — 36 tasks. Ireland: Item Fees flagged automatically
  • Restaurant (L-Series) — 22 tasks
  • Restaurant (K-Series) — 19 tasks
  • Accounting (Shogo) — 6 tasks
  • eCommerce (E-Series) — 14 tasks
  • Custom App — 6 tasks
  • Booxi — 19 tasks
  • Fore Connect — 7 tasks (sold as line item only)

Ecosystem Support is not a module. It shows as an info badge on the deal card only.

Progress tracking

Last updated: May 2026

Per-module progress

Each module shows its own progress bar: tasks completed ÷ total tasks in that module.

Overall progress (weighted)

Total completed tasks across all modules (incl. Step 0) ÷ total tasks across all modules. Modules with more tasks carry more weight.

Example: Golf (29 tasks, 75% done) + Retail (36 tasks, 100% done)
Done = 22 + 36 = 58   Total = 29 + 36 = 65
Overall = 58/65 = 89%

GO-LIVE tasks

Each module ends with a GO-LIVE task highlighted in green. When all modules reach GO-LIVE, the client is ready for handover to support.

Best practices

Last updated: May 2026
  • Never assume — schedule a check-in rather than guessing
  • Go-live date — always change in the hub, never directly in HubSpot
  • Ireland clients — Item Fees in Retail are flagged automatically. Always confirm before enabling
  • Keep hub up to date — mark tasks done as soon as they're complete
  • Credentials in 1Password only
  • Split training — Golf, Retail and F&B separately; managers separate from staff
